What the numbers mean
Tap any stat to sort the board by it.
🥇
Wins
First-place finishes. One winner per game.
📈
Win %
Wins ÷ games played, as a percentage.
⚔️
Players Beaten
Everyone who finished below you, summed across all games. Beating a 6-player table counts more than a 2-player one.
⚖️
Weighted Wins
Wins worth more for heavier games — Light ×0.5, Medium ×1, Heavy ×1.5. Rewards beating the brain-burners.
